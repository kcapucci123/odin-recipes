Chicken Terriyaki

Description

This sweet and tangy chicken-Terriyaki bowl is my go-to meal on a busy workday, because it is delicious and very easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • 1 block of Butter
  • 3 tbs of Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbs of Honey
  • 1 stalk of scallion finely chopped
  • some seasame seeds for garnish
  • Cooked rice
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cut Chicken Thighs into half inch strips
  2. Season strips with salt and pepper
  3. Heat butter in a pan and place Chicken Thighs until fully cooked
  4. In a bowl combine honey, soy sauce and a splash of water, mix
  5. Pour sauce into pan and reduce the sauce until it thickens
  6. Sprinkle scallion and seasame seeds and enjoy!
