Maruchan Ramen Noodles

Description

This super easy Ramen Noodles dish is hearty and its the perfect dish for a cold winter day

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of Maruchan ramen noodles
  • Finely chopped scallion
  • 1 hard-boiled egg

Steps

  1. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil
  2. Add ramen noodles and seasoning packets per instructions on label
  3. Sprinkle chopped scallion and top with hard-boiled egg
  4. Enjoy!
